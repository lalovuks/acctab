Our Services

Accounting

Acctab's accounting services involve preparing, reviewing, and maintaining accurate financial records to ensure businesses have a clear and reliable picture of their financial position. These services support informed decision-making by producing compliant statements, tracking financial performance, and giving clients timely insights into their operations.

Payroll

Acctab's payroll services manage the entire salary administration process, ensuring that employees are paid accurately, on time, and in accordance with statutory requirements. This includes handling deductions, taxes, compliance reporting, and payroll records so businesses can reduce administrative burden and avoid costly errors.

Get More Details

Bookkeeping

Acctab's bookkeeping services provide systematic recording and organization of daily financial transactions, ensuring that all income, expenses, and financial movements are captured correctly. By maintaining clean and up-to-date books, businesses gain the foundation needed for smooth audits, accurate reporting, and better financial management.

Accounting Advisory Services

Acctab's accounting advisory services help businesses interpret and apply complex accounting standards, ensuring financial statements are accurate, compliant, and aligned with regulatory frameworks. These services also guide clients through specialized transactions—such as mergers, restructures, or new reporting requirements—so they can make confident financial decisions.

Tax Services

Acctab provides comprehensive tax services that include tax planning, compliance, and advisory to help businesses meet their legal obligations while optimizing their tax position. The firm ensures clients remain fully aligned with evolving tax regulations, reducing risk and identifying opportunities for savings.

Company Secretarial Services

Acctab's company secretarial services ensure that businesses meet all corporate governance and statutory filing requirements, maintaining full compliance with local company laws. This includes maintaining company records, preparing resolutions, managing annual returns, and supporting directors with regulatory guidance to keep the business legally sound and well-organized.
